Project "CryptoSphere" transforms raw market data into a visually dynamic sphere that encapsulates the volatility and sentiment of the Bitcoin market.
The sphere's size is directly influenced by Bitcoin's market capitalization, serving as a tangible representation of its financial magnitude.
The coloration of the sphere is based on a logarithmic regression, plotting Bitcoin's price against time but colored in bands that represent different market sentiments, from undervalued to overvalued regions.
Morphing intensity is driven by daily price changes, visualizing the market's volatility.
Through this interactive and immersive visualization, the project aims to offer an engaging and intuitive way to perceive complex financial data, providing insights into the ever-changing landscape of the cryptocurrency market.
